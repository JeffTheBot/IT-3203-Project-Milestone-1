About the Author

Jeff A. Camacho is a U.S. Marine Corps veteran and Network Administrator from Powder Springs, Georgia. He specializes in network performance, design, and troubleshooting across enterprise systems. Jeff completed advanced training through the Network Admin Pathway at Intellectual Point (CompTIA Security+, CCNA, and CCNP) and is currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at Kennesaw State University.

His professional background includes nearly a decade of service in the U.S. Marine Corps, where he supervised and mentored over 40 technicians, managed large-scale tactical communications systems, and maintained mission-critical networks supporting $10 million worth of equipment. Jeff brings a passion for technology, leadership, and continuous learning into his academic and professional growth.

Abstract

This site adapts my paper on how HTML began, the role it plays in structuring web content, how it evolved into a living standard, and where it is headed. It summarizes the origins with Tim Berners-Lee, the milestone versions (1.0 through 4.01 and XHTML), and the major leap to HTML5 with native multimedia and semantic elements. It also explains why HTML focuses on meaning and structure while CSS and JavaScript provide presentation and behavior.
